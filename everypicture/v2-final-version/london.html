A cozy London brunch

Comfort level: 10/10

Tastiness: 9/10

There's something special about eating with a skyline view, especially in london! The perfectly cooked egg, the crispy waffle, all the little touches really made my london trip way more special.

The atmosphere was warm and inviting, making it the perfect spot to start a busy day of exploring the cold winter city.

Me and my sisters were estatic to come here after hearing many of our friends recommend it so we were very excited to try it for ourselves!
