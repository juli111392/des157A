A special birthday dinner at Nobu

Comfort level: 10/10

Tastiness: 7/10

After a long day riding ATVs and shopping, my last night in Miami ended at Nobu.

My favourite thing about the dinner was when I got surprised with a cake on fire!! seems like they really like fire at nobu...

Dinner was very close and full of laughter, though the food may be overrated I think everyone should go there at least once!
