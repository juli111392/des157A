How to Play

Goal: Be the first player to reach 20 points and collect yummy sushi!

How it works:

  • Roll the dice to collect sushi and earn points
  • Each roll adds points directly to your score
  • Click PASS to end your turn and save your sushi haul
  • GOLD SUSHI! Roll a 6 to get the legendary golden sushi - the most valuable catch!
  • OH NO! Roll a 1 and you'll hit the spicy wasabi bomb! You'll lose ALL your points and your turn ends!
  • Be strategic - keep rolling to collect more sushi, or play it safe and pass!

At the end: See all the sushi you collected on your journey to victory!

First player to reach 20 points wins! Good luck, sushi chef!
